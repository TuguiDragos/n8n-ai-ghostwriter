## 📖 Overview

**n8n AI Ghostwriter** is an autonomous AI blog engine built entirely in [n8n](https://n8n.io). It turns a single niche into a self-running content operation: it finds a fresh topic, researches it with real cited sources, writes and fact-checks the article, generates a cover image, drafts platform-ready social posts, and publishes everything to your blog and your social channels. The only manual step is a single approval click.

Hiring a researcher, a writer, a fact-checker, a designer, and a social manager is slow and expensive. This runs all five roles for you, on a schedule, for the cost of API credit. It is hands-free content marketing that still reads like it was made with care.

### What it does

1. Picks a fresh, non-duplicate topic in your niche.
2. Researches it with 4 to 7 current, cited sources.
3. Writes a roughly 1,000 word article and fact-checks every claim.
4. Generates an art-directed cover image.
5. Drafts platform-specific social posts, and fact-checks those too.
6. Waits for your one-click approval.
7. Publishes to Ghost and distributes across your social channels.

### Why it is different

This is not a "prompt to blog post" toy. It is an editorial system with the same controls a serious newsroom would demand:

- **No hallucinated sources.** Every dossier passes hard gates: a minimum source count, tier-one source requirements, domain diversity, and an independence rule so a claim never rests on a single vendor.
- **Claim-level fact-checking.** A separate model reads the finished draft and counts how many claims are actually supported. A failing draft is automatically repaired, not shipped. The social posts go through their own validate-and-repair loop.
- **It never repeats itself.** Before choosing a topic, it reads recent titles and steers away from ground it has already covered.
- **A human is always the last word.** Nothing publishes without your explicit approval.

The product is a chain of four sequential workflows plus a dedicated error handler, connected through one Notion database that acts as the shared state machine.

</p>

<p align="center">

The article moves through a clear set of states in Notion:

```

                                  (failed validation) -> Failed
```

## 🚀 Built to scale

The pipeline is modular by design. It is not locked to a fixed setup, and it is not limited to a handful of channels.

- **Add more platforms in minutes.** Because the social layer posts through [Buffer](https://buffer.com), you can connect any network Buffer supports, such as LinkedIn, Instagram, Pinterest, or TikTok, and clone a single node to start posting there. It is not limited to three or four platforms.
- **Already proven across many networks.** Across its streams the engine already publishes to Facebook, Threads, X, LinkedIn, Pinterest, and Bluesky.
- **Already running more than one stream.** The same system runs two independent research streams in production at once, Collateral Ledger and Expansion Ledger, each with its own niche and its own database, feeding the same blog. One engine, many streams.
- **Swap any part.** Change the model, swap the CMS, adjust the schedule, or add and remove channels, without rebuilding the system.

In short: start with one blog and a few channels, then grow to as many streams and platforms as you want on the same foundation.
